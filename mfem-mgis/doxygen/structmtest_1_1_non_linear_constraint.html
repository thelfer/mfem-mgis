enum mtest::NonLinearConstraint::NormalisationPolicy

define the normalisation policy

Enumerator
DRIVINGVARIABLECONSTRAINT 

this policy assumes that the constraint is defined by a function which values are of the order of magnitude of the driving variables.

THERMODYNAMICFORCECONSTRAINT 

this policy assumes that the constraint is defined by a function which values are of the order of magnitude of the thermodynamic forces.
