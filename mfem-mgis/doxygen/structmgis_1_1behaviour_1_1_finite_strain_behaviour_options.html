enum mgis::behaviour::FiniteStrainBehaviourOptions::StressMeasure

stress measure requested for finite strain behaviours

The first Piola-Kirchhoff stress \(P\) is related to the Cauchy stress \(\sigma\) by: \f[ ￼￼P = J \,\cdot\,\sigma\,F^{-T} \f] where \f(F\) is the deformation gradient and \(J\) its determinant.

Note
some authors use a different convention to define the first Piola-Kirchhoff stress using the transpose of \(P\)
