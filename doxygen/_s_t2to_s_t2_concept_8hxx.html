◆ norm()

TFEL_HOST_DEVICE constexpr auto tfel::math::norm ( const ST2toST2Concept auto &  A)
constexprnoexcept

This function computes the norm of a st2tost2 as.

\[ ||A||=\sqrt{\frac{1}{N}{}^tA::A}\]

where

\[N\]

is the size of the tensor (for example,

\[6\]

for a dimension 3, and

\[4\]

for a dimension 2).

\[ ||A||=\sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i,j,k,l}A_{ijkl}A_{ijkl}}\]

or also

\[ ||A||=\sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i,j} A_{ij}A_{ij}}\]

in Voigt notation. The factor

\[\frac 1N\]

ensures that the norm of

\[I\]

is 1.

Returns
the norm
Parameters
[in]Ast2tost2

\[ A::B=\sum_{i,j} A_{ij}B_{ji}\]

in Voigt notation, which also corresponds to

\[ A::B=\sum_{i,j,k,l}A_{ijkl}B_{lkji}=\sum_{i,j,k,l}A_{ijkl}B_{klij}\]

\[ Tr\left(A\right)=\sum_i A_{ii}\]

in Voigt notation, which also corresponds to

\[ Tr\left(A\right)=I::A=\frac{1}{2}\sum_{i,j}(A_{ijij}+A_{ijji})\]
