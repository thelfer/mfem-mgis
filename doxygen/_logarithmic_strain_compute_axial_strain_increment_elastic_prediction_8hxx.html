This method computes an elastic prediction of the increment of the total logarithmic axial strain in axisymmetrical generalised plane strain.

This increment \(\Delta\varepsilon_{zz}^{\mathrm{to,log}}\) satisfies this following equation:

\[ \theta\,D_{11}\,\Delta\varepsilon_{zz}^{\mathrm{to,log}}+ \theta\,D_{10}\,\Delta\varepsilon_{rr}^{\mathrm{to,log}}+ \theta\,D_{12}\,\Delta\varepsilon_{\theta\theta}^{\mathrm{to,log}}= \exp\left( \left.\varepsilon_{zz}^{\mathrm{to,log}}\right|_{t}+ \theta\,\Delta\varepsilon_{zz}^{\mathrm{to,log}} \right)\,\Pi_{zz} -\left.T_{zz}\right|_{t} \]

where \(\left.T_{zz}\right|_{t}\) is the value of the logarithmic stress at the beginning of the time step. \(\left.T_{zz}\right|_{t}\) is computed as follows:

\[ \left.T_{zz}\right|_{t} = D_{11}\,\left.\varepsilon_{zz}^{\mathrm{el}}\right|_{t}+ D_{10}\,\left.\varepsilon_{rr}^{\mathrm{el}}\right|_{t}+ D_{12}\,\left.\varepsilon_{\theta\theta}^{\mathrm{el}}\right|_{t} \]
