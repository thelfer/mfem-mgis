Detailed Description

template<tfel::math::ScalarConcept StressType>
requires (tfel::math::checkUnitCompatibility< tfel::math::unit::Stress, StressType>())
struct tfel::material::homogenization::elasticity::OrientedDistribution< StressType >

This struct represents a distribution of inclusions with a unique orientation in 3d, which can be only Spheroids or Ellipsoids. The vectors \(n_a\) and \(n_b\) define the orientation. The first semi-length of the inclusion corresponds to \(n_a\). The second semi-length corresponds to \(n_b\).
