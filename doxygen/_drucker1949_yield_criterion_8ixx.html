const Drucker1949BaseType< StressStensor >  c

compute the Drucker1949 yield stress defined by:

\[ \sigma_{\mathrm{eq}}^{D}= \sqrt{3}\,\sqrt[6]{J_{2}^{3} - c\,J_{3}^{2}} \]

where (J_{2}) and (J_{3}) are the first and second invariants of the deviatoric part of the stress tensor
