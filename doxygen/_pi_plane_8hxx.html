this function project a stress state defined by the the principal stress axes \(\left(S_{0},S_{1},S_{2}\right)\) on the \(\pi\)-plane, which is defined by:

\[ S_{0}+S_{1}+S_{2}=0 \]

A basis of the \(\pi\)-plane is defined by the following vectors:

\[ \vec{n}_{0}= \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\, \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix} \quad\text{and}\quad \vec{n}_{1}= \frac{1}{\sqrt{6}}\, \begin{pmatrix} -1 \\ -1 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix} \]
