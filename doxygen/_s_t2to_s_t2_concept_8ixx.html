constexprnoexcept

This function computes the norm of a st2tost2 as.

\[ ||A||=\sqrt{{}^tA::A}\]

.

\[ ||A||=\sqrt{\sum_{i,j,k,l}A_{ijkl}A_{ijkl}}\]

or also

\[ ||A||=\sqrt{\sum_{i,j} A_{ij}A_{ij}}\]

in Voigt notation.

the norm

This function computes the quadruple contraction of two st2tost2 as.

\[ A::B=\sum_{i,j} A_{ij}B_{ji}\]

in Voigt notation, which also corresponds to

\[ A::B=\sum_{i,j,k,l}A_{ijkl}B_{lkji}=\sum_{i,j,k,l}A_{ijkl}B_{klij}\]

This function computes the trace of a st2tost2 as.

\[ Tr\left(A\right)=\sum_i A_{ii}\]

in Voigt notation, which also corresponds to

\[ Tr\left(A\right)=I::A=\frac{1}{2}\sum_{i,j}(A_{ijij}+A_{ijji})\]

the trace
