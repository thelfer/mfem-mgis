Detailed Description

class describing a Data isotropic hardening rule

\[ R\left(p\right)=R_{0}+\left(R_{\infty}-R_{0}\right)\,\exp\left(-b\,p\right) \]

where:

  • \(R_{0}\) is the yield strength
  • \(R_{inf}\) is the maximal yield strength
  • \(b\) is a parameter
