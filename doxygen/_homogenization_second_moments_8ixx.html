This function computes the averages on each phase of the square of the equivalent strain assuming a Hashin-Shtrikman homogenization scheme, and assuming two phases: spherical isotropic inclusions embedded in an isotropic matrix (note that the inclusion can be softer or stiffer than the matrix)
