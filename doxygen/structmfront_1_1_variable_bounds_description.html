Detailed Description

a structure in charge of describing bounds on a variable. Two kinds of bounds are considered:

  • physical bounds: give the bounds out of which this variable is not physically defined. For example, a temperature must be positive.
  • (standard) bounds: give the bounds out of which the correlation has not been identified.
