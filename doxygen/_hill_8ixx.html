[in]H_Northotropic coefficient This function builds the Hill Tensor defined by :

\[\left( \begin{array}{cccccc} H_F+H_H & -H_F & -H_H & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ -H_F & H_G+H_F & -H_G & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ -H_H & -H_G & H_H+H_G & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & H_L & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & H_M & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & H_N \\ \end{array} \right) \]

if \(\sigma\) is a second order symetric tensor (stensor), \(\sigma|H_H*\sigma\) computes the Hill stress :

\[ H_F\left(\sigma_{11}-\sigma_{22}\right)^2+H_G\left(\sigma_{22}-\sigma_{33}\right)^2+H_H\left(\sigma_{33}-\sigma_{11}\right)^2 + 2H_L\sigma_{12}^{2} + 2H_M\sigma_{13}^{2} + 2H_N\sigma_{23}^{2} \]

Warning
This convention is given in the book of Lemaître et Chaboche and seems to differ from the one described in most other books.
Returns
