Detailed Description

class describing a power isotropic hardening rule

\[ R\left(p\right)=R_{0}\left(p+p_{0}\right)^{n} \]

where:

  • \(R_{0}\) is the yield strength
  • \(n\) is the exponent
  • \(p_{0}\) is a parameter which avoids an infinite derivative of the previous relation for (p=0) in (n\leq 1).
