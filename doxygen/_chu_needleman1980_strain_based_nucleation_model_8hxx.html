computes the porosity rate factor \(A_{n}\) as defined by the nucleation model of Chu and Needleman.

\[ A_{n} = \frac{f_N}{s_N \sqrt{2\pi}} \exp\left(-\frac{1}{2} \left(\frac{p - \epsilon_N}{s_N}\right)^2\right) \]

Chu, C. C., and A. Needleman. 1980. “Void Nucleation Effects in Biaxially Stretched Sheets.” Journal of Engineering Materials and Technology 102 (3): 249–56

computes the porosity rate factor \(A_{n}\) as defined by the nucleation model of Chu and Needleman and its derivative \(\frac{\partial A_{n}}{\partial p}\) with respect to the equivalent plastic strain \(p\).

\[ A_{n} = \frac{f_N}{s_N \sqrt{2\pi}} \exp\left(-\frac{1}{2} \left(\frac{p - \epsilon_N}{s_N}\right)^2\right) \]

Chu, C. C., and A. Needleman. 1980. “Void Nucleation Effects in Biaxially Stretched Sheets.” Journal of Engineering Materials and Technology 102 (3): 249–56
