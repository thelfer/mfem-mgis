This function builds the strain localisation tensor of a general ellipsoid with a general elasticity, embedded in an isotropic matrix. The localisation tensor \(A\) is defined as follows :

\[\epsilon = A:E_0\]

where \(E_0\) is the uniform strain tensor imposed at infinity, and \(\epsilon\) is the strain tensor solution of Eshelby problem for the ellipsoid. The ellipsoid also has a specific orientation given by the vectors \(n_a\), \(n_b\), which provides a direct local basis \((n_a,n_b,n_c)\). The elasticity \(C_i\) is specified in this local basis.

Returns
an object of type st2tost2, which is the fourth-order localisation tensor \(A\) (in the global basis).
