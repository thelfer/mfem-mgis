This function gives the Hashin-Shtrikman bounds for a d-dimensional isotropic composite composed of N isotropic phases. The formulas can be found in Torquato (2002).

Template Parameters
realunderlying type
StressTypetype of the elastic constants related to the phases
Nnumber of phases
ddimension (2 or 3)
Returns
an object of type std::pair<KGModuli<StressType>, KGModuli<StressType>>. First element of the pair is the lower bound and second element is the upper bound. Each bound is KGModuli with bulk and shear moduli.
Parameters
[in]tab_fstd::array<real,N> of volumic fractions of phases
[in]tab_Kstd::array<StressType,N> of bulk moduli of phases
[in]tab_mustd::array<StressType,N> of shear moduli of phases
