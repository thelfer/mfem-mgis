compute the decomposition of a symmetric tensor in a positive and negative parts and their derivatives.

Template Parameters
DPPType: type in which derivative of the positive part is stored
DNPType: type in which derivative of the negative part is stored
PPType: type of positive part of the symmetric tensor
NPType: type of negative part of the symmetric tensor
StensorType: type of the symmetric tensor
Parameters
[out]dpp: derivative of the positive part of the symmetric tensor
[out]dnp: derivative of the negative part of the symmetric tensor
[out]pp: positive part of the symmetric tensor
[out]np: positive part of the symmetric tensor
[in]s: symmetric tensor
[in]eps: numerical precision used to decipher if two eigenvalues are equal
Precondition
DPPType must implement the ST2toST2Concept
DNPType must implement the ST2toST2Concept
PPType must implement the StensorConcept
NPType must implement the StensorConcept
StensorType must implement the StensorConcept
