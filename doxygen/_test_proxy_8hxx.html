◆ TFEL_PP_JOIN

#define TFEL_PP_JOIN (   X,
  Y 
)    TFEL_PP_DO_JOIN(X, Y)

this macro joins joins its two arguments together.

This macro was taken from the boost library:

  • http://boost.org/

The following piece of macro magic joins the two arguments together, even when one of the arguments is itself a macro (see 16.3.1 in C++ standard). The key is that macro expansion of macro arguments does not occur in TFEL_PP_DO_JOIN2 but does in TFEL_PP_DO_JOIN.
