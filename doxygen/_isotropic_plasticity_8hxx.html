◆ computeJ3Derivative()

TFEL_HOST_DEVICE constexpr auto tfel::material::computeJ3Derivative ( const tfel::math::StensorConcept auto &  s)
constexpr
Returns
the derivative of the determinant of the deviator of symmetric tensor.

Let \(\underline{s}\) be a symmetric tensor and \(J_{3}\) be the determinant of \(\underline{s}'\) the deviator of \(\underline{s}\):

\[ J_{3} = \mathrm{det}\left(\underline{s}'\right) = \mathrm{det}\left(\underline{s}-\mathrm{tr}\left(\underline{s}'\right)\,\underline{I}\right) \]

This function computes \(\displaystyle\frac{\partial J_{3}}{\partial \underline{\sigma}}\).

Parameters
[in]stensor
Note
this function is a simple wrapper around the tfel::math::computeDeviatorDeterminantDerivative function.

TFEL_HOST_DEVICE constexpr auto tfel::material::computeJ3SecondDerivative ( const tfel::math::StensorConcept auto &  s)
constexpr

Let \(\underline{s}\) be a symmetric tensor and \(J_{3}\) be the determinant of \(\underline{s}'\) the deviator of \(\underline{s}\):

\[ J_{3} = \mathrm{det}\left(\underline{s}'\right) = \mathrm{det}\left(\underline{s}-\mathrm{tr}\left(\underline{s}'\right)\,\underline{I}\right) \]

This function computes \(\displaystyle\frac{\partial^{2} J_{3}}{\partial \underline{\sigma}^{2}}\).

Parameters
[in]stensor
Returns
the second derivative of determinant of the deviator of a symmetric tensor with respect to this tensor.
Note
this function is a simple wrapper around the tfel::math::computeDeviatorDeterminantSecondDerivative function.
