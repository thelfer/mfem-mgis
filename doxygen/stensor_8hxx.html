◆ computeDeviatorDeterminantDerivative()

TFEL_HOST_DEVICE constexpr auto tfel::math::computeDeviatorDeterminantDerivative ( const StensorConcept auto &  s)
constexprnoexcept
Returns
the derivative of the determinant of the deviator of symmetric tensor.

Let \(\underline{s}\) be a symmetric tensor and \(J_{3}\) be the determinant of \(\underline{s}'\) the deviator of \(\underline{s}\):

\[ J_{3} = \mathrm{det}\left(\underline{s}'\right) = \mathrm{det}\left(\underline{s}-\mathrm{tr}\left(\underline{s}'\right)\,\underline{I}\right) \]

This function computes \(\displaystyle\frac{\partial J_{3}}{\partial \underline{\sigma}}\).

Parameters
[in]stensor where the the determinant is evaluated
